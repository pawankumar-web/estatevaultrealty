About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Property, Locally and Globally.

EstateVault Realty is one of India’s pioneering Integrated Property Consultants (IPC), recognized for its strategic vision, industry expertise, and strong professional network.

Since our inception in 2012, we've built a strong footprint not only across India but also in the GCC and European markets, offering best-in-class real estate solutions tailored to the needs of today’s dynamic clients.

At EstateVault Realty, we are committed to conducting business with integrity, transparency, and ethical excellence. Our core values ensure that every client interaction — whether with investors, partners, or stakeholders — is built on trust and long-term value.
